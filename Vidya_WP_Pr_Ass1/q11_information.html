DYP ACS College Pimpri

Dr. D. Y. Patil Arts, Commerce & Science College was established in the year 1995 and it is a distinguished degree college affiliated to the University of Pune. Sheltered in huge well architectured premises along with multiple institutes, the college has a unique academic atmosphere chiefly characterized by interdisciplinary scholarly interaction and healthy competitive spirit and enthusiasm to excel in various curricular activities. The college has been Accredited by NAAC with A++ Grade.
